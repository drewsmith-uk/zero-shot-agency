# Backlink Reconnaissance

## Objective
Identify where top AI agencies and AI developer tools are getting their mentions, citations, and backlinks to dominate the Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) space.

## Current Landscape Analysis
Top agencies frequently gain mentions and backlinks through:
1. **Developer Hubs:** GitHub repositories, HuggingFace model cards, and documentation portals.
2. **AI Newsletters:** Ben's Bites, TLDR AI, and The Rundown AI frequently link to innovative AI tools and case studies.
3. **Directories & Aggregators:** Product Hunt, 'There's an AI for that', and specialized AI tool directories.
4. **Community Platforms:** Reddit (r/LocalLLaMA, r/MachineLearning), Hacker News, and Discord communities.

## Actionable Targets for Zero-Shot Agency
1. **Open Source Contributions:** Publish specialized agents or tools on GitHub/HuggingFace with links back to the agency's primary site in the READMEs.
2. **Newsletter Outreach:** Submit case studies of successful AI agent deployments to top AI newsletters.
3. **Directory Listings:** Ensure Zero-Shot Agency's tools are listed in key AI directories with high domain authority.
4. **Educational Content:** Produce high-quality technical write-ups (like this wiki) and share them on platforms like Hacker News and dev.to.
